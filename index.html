ABOUT COVID-19

COVID-19 is a highly infectious sickness. The virus originated in a chinese village called Wuhan. A chinese man decided it was a great idea to eat a bat. This resulted in the massive outbreak of COVID-19. COVID-19 is basically a cold but way more infectious, some symptoms involve coughing, runny nose and a fever.

The chinese government is only just now deciding to lift the lockdown. The virus has already gone global and China is starting to recover.

COVID-19 CHARACTERISTICS

  1. Fast spreading, mass infection rate
  2. Symptoms of a cold: coughing, fever, sneezing, runny nose
  3. Corona originated in Wuhan,China
